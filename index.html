Taco Trivia

When did tacos first appear in the United Stated?

Jeffrey M. Picher, taco historiam and professor of history at the University of Minnestoa, says the earliest mention of tacos in the United States are in a newspaper from 1905. (Source: Smithsonian Magazine)
